The Grapes

Chardonnay

Chardonnay is a white wine with high acidity. The main Grape is produced in France..

Cabernet Savignon

Cabernet Sauvignon is one of the world's most widely recognized red wine grape varieties.

Merlot

Merlot is a dark blue-colored wine grape variety, that is used as both a blending grape and for varietal wines.

Syrah

Syrah, also known as Shiraz, is a dark-skinned grape variety grown throughout the world and used primarily to produce red wine.

Pinot Noir

Pinot noir is a red wine grape variety of the species Vitis vinifera. The name may also refer to wines created predominantly from pinot noir grapes.

Savignon Blanc

Sauvignon blanc is a green-skinned grape variety that originates from the Bordeaux region of France. It is possibly a descendant of Savagnin.
